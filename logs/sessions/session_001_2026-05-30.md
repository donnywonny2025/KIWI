### Key Discoveries
1. **ext_tune(freq, mode, zoom)** is the ONLY reliable tuning method.
2. Clicking the frequency input field causes double-input/appending — DO NOT USE.
3. Mode must match signal type — LSB on AM station = garbled.
4. WWV 5 MHz is in skip zone from Michigan at night, but WWV 2.5 MHz propagates strongly (although drowned by local power-line noise).
5. Local AM broadcast (WOOD 1300) pulls strong ground wave at 25 miles.
6. **Waterfall sensitivity offsets**: If floor/ceil offsets are set too narrow (like 0 to +5 dB), the entire waterfall paints pitch black because signal values are negative. Setting ceil to +15 dB and floor to -25 dB restores full spectrum visibility.
7. **Connection preservation**: When the WebSocket hangs, a simple `goto_url` page reload restores audio and scrolling.
